A Few Words About Me

I am student at the University of Northern Colorado double-majoring in Business Management and Marketing. I am extremely involved in student life on my campus and I am interested in pursuing a career in entrepreneurship. I enjoy playing basketball, reading, traveling, and spending time outdoors.

Clubs and Activities

Outside of my classes, I am currently involved in the Collegiate Entrepreneurs' Organization (CEO), Delta Sigma Pi, and Christian Challenge. In CEO, I serve as the chapter president and lead our club as we manage our chapter business, Bear Box. In Delta Sigma Pi, I am VP of Chapter Operations, which means that I help keep our chapter on track with events and activities. Lastly, with Christian Challenge, I serve as the student president as well as a Bible study leader.

Career Interests

I am extremely passionate about entrepreneurship. I believe that entreprenership is the most powerful way for me to impact the world and community around me, and I have already seen that take place through my entrepreneurial efforts in the CEO club. In the future, I hope to be an entrepreneur and launch my own startup. To get there, I plan on working for a startup in a big city for a few years to truly learn entrepreneurship through a program called Venture for America.

Personal Life

Although I attend university in Colorado, I grew up in Kyiv, Ukraine, which is my home. I am engaged to my highschool sweetheart who also grew up in Ukraine and attends a US university. We're planning to get married in June of 2024 after we both graduate and start our life together in a big city somewhere. My hobbies include basketball, hiking/camping, reading books, and traveling.
